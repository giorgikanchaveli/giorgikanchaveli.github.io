Giorgi Kanchaveli

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at Bocconi University, advised by Hugo Lavenant and Marta Catalano.

Before starting my Ph.D., I obtained an M.Sc. in Statistics from the University of Turin and the Collegio Carlo Alberto, and a B.Sc. in Economics, Management and Computer Science from Bocconi University.

My research interests lie in mathematical statistics, particularly in its connections with optimal transport. I am also interested in Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods and machine learning.
